3 — Conversion Strategy

  1. Soft paywall: let users complete their first full scan free — the "wow" moment — then paywall the detailed report, routine, and tracking.
  2. Anchor weekly against yearly: always display "$5.99/week" beside "$39.99/year ($0.77/wk)". The contrast makes annual feel like a steal and lifts annual mix.
  3. 3-day free trial on the yearly plan only — drives trial starts; reminder push on day 2 reduces churn.
  4. Default-select the yearly plan on the paywall (higher LTV, lower refund/churn than weekly).
  5. Use RevenueCat to manage trials, pricing experiments, and App Store + Play subscriptions without backend work.
  6. Run price A/B tests post-launch ($4.99 vs $5.99 weekly; $39.99 vs $49.99 yearly) to find the revenue-maximizing point.
  7. Win-back offer: 50% off yearly to lapsed/cancelled users.
Projected positioning: At $39.99/yr ClearMaxx undercuts MDacne ($288/yr) and GlowScan's annualized cost, while matching category norms on the weekly impulse tier — competitive on price and superior on features (all-four-pillars + cause engine).
